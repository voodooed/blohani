LiDAR@Geoinformatics Laboratory • IIT Kanpur

Advancing Research and Innovation in LiDAR Technologies

For more than two decades, the LiDAR@Geoinformatics Laboratory at IIT Kanpur has conducted research in LiDAR sensing, point cloud processing, mapping, simulation, geospatial intelligence, and emerging applications of laser scanning technologies.

Our work spans airborne, mobile, and terrestrial laser scanning, supporting advances in geospatial science, infrastructure monitoring, environmental studies, autonomous systems, digital twins, and next-generation spatial intelligence.

Our Mandate

Advancing LiDAR Research Through Innovation, Collaboration, and Impact

Established at IIT Kanpur, the LiDAR@Geoinformatics Laboratory has been actively engaged in the development and application of LiDAR technologies across a wide range of domains. Over the years, the laboratory has contributed to advances in laser scanning, point cloud analytics, geospatial intelligence, and emerging sensing technologies through research, education, and field deployments.

This group brings together expertise spanning airborne, mobile, and terrestrial LiDAR systems, supporting interdisciplinary work in mapping, infrastructure assessment, environmental monitoring, digital twins, autonomous systems, and next-generation geospatial applications.

— Dr. Bharat Lohani, Principal Investigator
